Networking Prior to Data Collection

Over the years, I have found it invaluable to connect with other leaders and subject matter experts in the organization. These critical relationshipships inform what answers various departments and executives need, how processes run, and where to find that data.

I strongly believe that without these connections, data gathering and processing is severely limited or can be misguided. Listening is a crucial part of data analysis.
